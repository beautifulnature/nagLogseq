### [[1. Introduction to Fundamental Analysis]]

The Zerodha Varsity website offers an introduction to fundamental analysis (FA) for investors. The course explains that FA is a method for evaluating a company’s long-term financial health and performance. This involves analyzing the company’s financial statements, understanding the industry it operates in, and examining its business strategy. The course emphasizes that FA is a valuable tool for making investment decisions, particularly for those aiming to generate wealth over the long term. It also stresses that, while FA helps identify companies with strong fundamentals, technical analysis is needed for timing market entry and exit points. The author suggests a "Core Satellite Strategy" where a majority of the investment is allocated to fundamentally strong companies, while a smaller portion is used for active short-term trading. Finally, the course highlights that the tools needed for FA are simple and readily available, such as annual reports, industry data, news, and MS Excel.
