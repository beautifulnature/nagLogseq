	- Sumitomo Chemical India Ltd. (SCIL) is a prominent player in the agrochemical sector, operating as a subsidiary of Sumitomo Chemical Company Ltd. (SCCL), Japan. Established in India in 2000, SCIL has significantly expanded its presence in the agricultural and chemical industries, focusing on crop protection, biopesticides, feed additives, and environmental health products.## Company Overview
	  **Parent Company**: SCIL is part of Sumitomo Chemical Company Ltd., which has a long history dating back to 1913. The parent company is recognized for its strong research and development (R&D) capabilities, spending approximately 9% of its revenue on R&D activities[1](https://ashikagroup.com/images/blog_images/2022-05-02_9442_Ashika%20-%20Stock%20Picks%20%E2%80%93%20Sumitomo%20Chemical%20India%20Ltd.%20-%20May%202022.pdf)[2](https://in.linkedin.com/company/sumitomo-chemical-india-ltd).**Product Portfolio**: SCIL's product offerings include:
	- **Agrochemicals**: Insecticides, fungicides, herbicides, and plant growth regulators.
	- **Biopesticides**: Environmentally friendly pest control solutions.
	- **Feed Additives**: Products aimed at improving animal nutrition.
	- **Household Insecticides**: Solutions for pest control in domestic settings[1](https://ashikagroup.com/images/blog_images/2022-05-02_9442_Ashika%20-%20Stock%20Picks%20%E2%80%93%20Sumitomo%20Chemical%20India%20Ltd.%20-%20May%202022.pdf)[3](https://www.sharescart.com/article/sumitomo-chemical-india-limited).
	  
	  The company operates through three primary segments: Industrial Chemicals, Agro Chemicals, and Public Health Chemicals. It has five manufacturing facilities located primarily in Gujarat and Maharashtra, along with a network of branches and depots across India[1](https://ashikagroup.com/images/blog_images/2022-05-02_9442_Ashika%20-%20Stock%20Picks%20%E2%80%93%20Sumitomo%20Chemical%20India%20Ltd.%20-%20May%202022.pdf)[2](https://in.linkedin.com/company/sumitomo-chemical-india-ltd).## Market Presence and Growth
	  SCIL has established a robust market position not only in India but also in international markets, including Africa. Approximately 20% of its sales come from exports[1](https://ashikagroup.com/images/blog_images/2022-05-02_9442_Ashika%20-%20Stock%20Picks%20%E2%80%93%20Sumitomo%20Chemical%20India%20Ltd.%20-%20May%202022.pdf). The company is actively involved in developing new products and expanding its portfolio through backward integration and partnerships with its parent company. This strategy enhances its ability to introduce proprietary products tailored to local markets while leveraging global innovations[3](https://www.sharescart.com/article/sumitomo-chemical-india-limited).## Financial Performance
	  As of May 2022, SCIL reported a market capitalization of approximately ₹21,460 crore. The company's financial health is characterized by a consistent increase in net profit margins and earnings per share (EPS), indicating strong operational performance and profitability[1](https://ashikagroup.com/images/blog_images/2022-05-02_9442_Ashika%20-%20Stock%20Picks%20%E2%80%93%20Sumitomo%20Chemical%20India%20Ltd.%20-%20May%202022.pdf). The company has also announced significant capital expenditure plans aimed at expanding its production capabilities for proprietary molecules, with an expected initial revenue potential of ₹2-5 billion from these new products[1](https://ashikagroup.com/images/blog_images/2022-05-02_9442_Ashika%20-%20Stock%20Picks%20%E2%80%93%20Sumitomo%20Chemical%20India%20Ltd.%20-%20May%202022.pdf)[3](https://www.sharescart.com/article/sumitomo-chemical-india-limited).## Challenges and Risks
	  Despite its strong market position, SCIL faces several challenges:
	- **Input Cost Fluctuations**: Rising raw material costs can adversely affect profit margins.
	- **Regulatory Risks**: Potential bans on certain products (e.g., Glyphosate) could impact revenue streams.
	- **Environmental Factors**: Droughts or adverse weather conditions can reduce agricultural output, leading to decreased demand for agrochemical products[1](https://ashikagroup.com/images/blog_images/2022-05-02_9442_Ashika%20-%20Stock%20Picks%20%E2%80%93%20Sumitomo%20Chemical%20India%20Ltd.%20-%20May%202022.pdf)[3](https://www.sharescart.com/article/sumitomo-chemical-india-limited).
	- ## Conclusion
	  Sumitomo Chemical India Ltd. is well-positioned for growth within the agrochemical industry due to its strong parentage, diverse product portfolio, and strategic focus on innovation and sustainability. While it faces certain risks inherent to the agricultural sector, its ongoing investments in R&D and expansion into new markets are likely to bolster its competitive edge in the coming years.
